AI/ML Engineer - Job Description

We are looking for an AI/ML Engineer with strong Python skills and experience in
natural language processing. Familiarity with TensorFlow or PyTorch is required.
Experience with FastAPI or Flask for building REST APIs is a plus. Knowledge of
vector databases like FAISS and embedding models (SBERT, BERT) is desired.
Experience with SQL databases and Docker is beneficial. 2+ years of experience
preferred. Familiarity with computer vision (OpenCV) and cloud platforms (AWS,
Azure, or Oracle Cloud Infrastructure) is a strong plus.
